Bomb

If you unveil it, start running because it is going to explode.

Game

For every matched pair of cards you get 1 point. Everytime you fail to match a pair your error counter will increase. The progress bar will show how close you are to winning the game. You will earn a place in the ranking if you win with less errors.

Bomb

The bomb will be hidden from you depending on what level you are playing. If you unveil it your game will restart but you will keep your error counter.

Difficulty

You have a "Help" button to reveal all cards for 2 seconds. Works only once per game. You win at 7 points.

The bomb will be revealed to you for 2 seconds at the start of the game. You win at 7 points.

The cards will not be revealed to you at the start of the game. You win at 6 points.

The bomb will be revealed to you for 1 second at the start of the game. If you fail more than 2 times you will lose. You win at 4 points.
